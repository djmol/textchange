About us

Hello Students, WPU Textbook exchange is here to serve you. We understand your pain and agony when it comes to purchasing textbooks. We are a group of students who have gone through a great amount of challenges when buying and selling used textbooks. We have created this site so everyone can exchange textbooks locally on campus. If you have any questions regarding the website, please contact us. We hope that this website improves your experience of purchasing and selling books.
